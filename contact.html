Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details and confirm your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including mobile money, bank transfers, and cash on delivery.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 1-3 business days within Accra, and 3-5 business days for other regions in Ghana.

Can I return products?

Yes, we accept returns for damaged or incorrect items within 7 days of delivery. Contact us for return instructions.

Do you ship outside Ghana?

Currently, we only ship within Ghana. We're working on expanding our delivery coverage to other countries.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS or email to track your delivery.
